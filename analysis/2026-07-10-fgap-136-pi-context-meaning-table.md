# TASK-110 meaning-gathering: pi-context internal-tracker-ID comment citations

## Key methodological finding

This package's substrate has unusually heavy ID-number reuse: the live `.context` substrate was stood up fresh (per live `DEC-0019`, 2026-06-27) as a "curated carry-forward" from a frozen predecessor (`.project-archived`), and many FGAP/DEC/TASK numbers got reassigned to unrelated new items post-carry-forward. Consequently a large fraction of citations resolve live but to the wrong item, requiring the archived fallback â€” and a handful of IDs are cited with two genuinely different meanings across different comment sites ("split" IDs below), because the same numeral was authoring-time-valid for two different gaps at two different points in this project's history.
